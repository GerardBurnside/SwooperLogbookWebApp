# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Swooper LogBook** ("we," "us," or "the App") handles your information. 

## 1. Data Collection and Storage
The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All data generated by the App is stored directly in your own Google Drive account within a Google Sheet created by the App.

## 2. Google Drive Access
To function correctly, the App requests access to your Google Drive. 
* The app uses this access solely to read, write, and sync your App data between your devices.
* The app does not have access to your other Google Drive files unless they were created by this App.
* We do not see, share, or sell your data.

## 3. Security
The App uses Google OAuth for authentication. We never see or store your Google password. Security of the data stored in your Google Drive is managed by Google.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
Although we have no plan to change this policy, your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
