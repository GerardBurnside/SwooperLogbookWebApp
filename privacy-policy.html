Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Swooper Logbook ("we," "us," or "the App") handles your information.

1. Data Collection and Storage

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All data generated by the App is stored directly in your own Google Drive account within a Google Sheet created by the App.

2. Google Drive Access

To function correctly across devices, the App requests access to your Google Drive via OAuth. We use this access solely to read, write, and sync your App data between your devices. We do not have access to your other Google Drive files unless they were created by this App, and we never share or sell your data.

3. Security

The App uses Google OAuth for authentication. We never see or store your Google password. Security of the data stored in your Google Drive is managed entirely by Google.
